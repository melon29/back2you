Stay Safe

🛡️ Safety Tips

Your safety matters just as much as reuniting the pet. Keep these in mind.

✔

Always verify ownership before handing over a pet.

✔

Meet in public, well-lit places.

✔

Avoid sharing sensitive personal information online.

✔

Take someone you trust with you when meeting a stranger.
